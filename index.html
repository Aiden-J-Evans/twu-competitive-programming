Team Meetings

The Trinity Western University ICPC Competitive Programming Team meets on Thursdays at 4:30 p.m. in the computer lab (NEU, A20). During these meetings, we are given a problem set to solve before the meeting ends.

We compete in the ICPC Pacific Northwest division and the next competition is November 16th, 2024 at TBD.

Previous Results

In ICPC 2023 Regionals, we had teams place 1st and 3rd, along with other strong placements.
